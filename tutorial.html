Ice Cupcakes Tutorial

Ice cupcakes are a fun and creative treat that's easy to make. Here's how:

  • Prepare cupcake batter using your favorite recipe.
  • Bake in preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for 20 minutes.
  • Let cupcakes cool before decorating with icing.
  • Use a piping bag to add colorful icing swirls on top.
  • Finish with sprinkles, edible glitter, or fondant decorations.


Decorative Cakes Tutorial

Decorative cakes are perfect for special occasions. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  • Start by baking your favorite cake in the desired shape and size.
  • Apply a crumb coat of buttercream and chill for 30 minutes.
  • Add a smooth layer of fondant or buttercream as a base.
  • Decorate with piping, fondant designs, and edible decorations.
  • Finish with toppers, flowers, or personalized messages.
